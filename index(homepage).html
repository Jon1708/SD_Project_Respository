Project Overview

F.R.O.G.S is a senior design project centered around the development of a reverse osmosis water desalination system designed for off-grid environments. The system operates entirely on solar power, enabling sustainable and independent water purification without reliance on traditional infrastructure.

Integrated within the device is an array of sensors used to monitor critical water quality metrics such as total dissolved solids (TDS) and temperature. This data is processed and presented to the user in real time, providing clear insight into water safety and overall system performance.
